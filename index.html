GDG Cloud Stockholm is a part of the Google Developers Group (GDG) organizations worldwide. We focus specifically on cloud technologies like Google Cloud and any other cloud tech out there, regardless of vendor. We want to foster community and spread knowledge above all else. We love to collaborate with people and hear their ideas on talks or events. So, please reach out if you are interested!
